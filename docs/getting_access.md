# Getting Access to U of A GenAI

## Limited Early Access Launch
Early access to the U of A GenAI tool will be limited to the first 100 users who request and are granted access. 

To request access:

1. Submit a completed [Early Access Request form](https://uarizona.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_5bscmtL8Pb7qidE) to receive a confirmation email of your access request.

2. Log into the [U of A GenAI platform](https://chat.ai2s.org/) (required for approval).

3. Once approved, you will receive an email with access info. Review the U of A GenAI Quick Start Guide.
Note: During the limited early access phase, some features, such as guardrails, might not be enabled or can change without notice.
