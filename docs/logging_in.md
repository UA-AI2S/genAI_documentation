# Logging In

Upon accessing https://chat.ai2s.org click the login button "Continue with cilogon (and select The University of Arizona)."

<img width="500" height="250" alt="orai" src="/../assets/logging_in.webp" />

<br>
<br>



You will be redirected to a CILogon page; Select The University of Arizona from the dropdown menu and click on the "Log On" button.

<img width="500" height="250" alt="orai" src="/../assets/cilogon.webp" />

<br>
<br>


You will be redirected to the familiar University of Arizona Web Auth system. Enter your credentials and click on "Login". You might need to approve the login with the Duo Push application.

<img width="500" height="250" alt="orai" src="/../assets/netid.webp" />
